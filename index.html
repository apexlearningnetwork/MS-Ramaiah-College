Admission Eligibility

Updated entry requirements across major programmes. Requirements are reviewed periodically by regulatory bodies — confirm your specific case with our counsellors.

MBBS

  • NEET UG qualified
  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum qualifying percentage as per NMC regulations

BDS

  • NEET UG qualified
  • PCB in Class 12

MD / MS / MDS

  • Relevant MBBS / BDS degree
  • NEET PG / NEET MDS qualification

Engineering

  • KCET qualified
  • COMEDK eligible
  • JEE Main score accepted

MBA

  • CAT / MAT / CMAT score
  • Karnataka PGCET eligible

Pharmacy

  • PCB or PCM eligibility as applicable
  • 10+2 with required science combination
